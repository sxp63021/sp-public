TITLE:  Lab - Sharing S3 Buckets across Accounts

=================================
S3 - Cross-Account Access
=================================
3 Different ways to share S3 buckets accross accounts
* Using Bucket Policies and IAM (applies across the entire bucket)
  Programmatic Access Only.
* Using Bucket ACLs & IAM (individual object). Programmatic Access Only.
* Cross-account IAM Roles. Programatic AND Console access.
