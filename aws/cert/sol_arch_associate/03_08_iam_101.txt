Key Features of IAM

Identity Access Management (IAM) offers the following features;

a. Centralised control of your AWS account
b. shared Access to your AWS account
c. Granular Permissions
d. Identity Federation (Including Active Directory, Facebook, Linkedin, etc)
e. MFA (Multi Factor Authentication)
f. Provide Temporary Access for users/devices and services where necessary
g. Allows you to setup your own password rotation policy
h. Integrates with many different AWS services
i. Supports PCI DSS Compliance
   Q. What is PCI DSS Compliance?
   A. This is a compliance that is needed for Credit Card Payment system
